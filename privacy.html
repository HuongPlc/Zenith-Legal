Privacy Policy

Last Updated: 28/02/2026

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how The Human Axis handles information when you use Zenith.

2. Information We Collect

a. Personal Information

We do not collect personal information such as names, email addresses, or phone numbers.

b. Automatically Collected Information

We use Firebase services provided by Google LLC for analytics and crash reporting. Firebase may collect:

  • Device type
  • Operating system version
  • App usage statistics
  • Crash reports
  • Anonymous identifiers

This information does not directly identify you.

c. In-App Purchase Information

In-app purchases are processed by Apple Inc. We do not receive or store your payment information. Apple may provide anonymized subscription status data necessary to validate access to premium features.

3. How We Use Information

  • Improve app performance
  • Fix bugs and crashes
  • Analyze feature usage
  • Verify subscription status
  • Ensure security and prevent abuse

We do not sell your data to third parties.

4. Data Sharing

We may share limited information with:

  • Firebase (analytics and crash reporting)
  • Apple (payment processing)
  • Authorities if required by law

5. Data Retention

Analytics and diagnostic data are retained only as long as necessary for operational and legal purposes.

6. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to access, correct, or request deletion of your data. You may contact us to exercise these rights.

7. Children’s Privacy

Zenith is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children.

8. Security

We implement reasonable technical and organizational safeguards to protect information. However, no system is completely secure.

9. Changes to Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Updates will be posted on this page.
